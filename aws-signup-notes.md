# AWS Account Creation Learning

## Step-by-Step Process

1. Opened AWS website for account creation.
2. Entered email ID, password, and account name.
3. Verified email and phone number.
4. Selected Personal account type.
5. Added billing information.
6. Tried UPI AutoPay for payment verification.
7. UPI mandate creation failed.
8. Switched to Visa debit card.
9. Entered card details and completed OTP verification.
10. Payment method was successfully submitted for verification.
11. AWS account setup completed successfully.

---

# Difficulties Faced

1. UPI mandate creation failed.
2. Debit card verification failed initially.
3. International transaction issue on debit card.
4. Confusion regarding billing verification.

---

# Solutions

1. Switched from UPI AutoPay to Visa debit card.
2. Enabled international/online transactions on the card.
3. Retried payment verification carefully.

---

# Learnings

1. AWS requires payment verification even for Free Tier.
2. International transaction support is important for AWS verification.
3. Billing alerts are important to avoid unexpected charges.
4. AWS account setup requires careful payment verification.
